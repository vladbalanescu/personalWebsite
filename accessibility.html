Accessibilty Statement

I am completely committed to make my personal website accessible to a large variety of visitors. The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) has produced guidelines for making web content accessible to people with disabilities. Pages within my personal website are designed to meet a general standard of AA compliance level from Web Content Accessibility Guidline (WCAG) 2.0. I am actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of my website, for being accessible to everyone, regardless of technology, ability or age.
