5th Year Projects

  • Technology used: Python, OpenCV, ImageJ, CellProfiler

  • Darwin Project - Antisocial Cells, quantifying the disruptors
    An year long research project in which we studied the interaction between healthy and abnormal cells from human bladder. The macroscopic functions of human bodily tissues is the product of the behaviours and interactions of their constituent cells on the microscopic level. When even a minimal proportion of these cells deviates from whatare the “standard” procedures of cell division, the collective outcome could be in the form of a potentially fatal disease such as cancer. The aim of this project was to identify and quantify the behaviours of these two categories of cells by employing image processing and ML.
